Mental Health

IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Serenity/Mind

It is a state of emotional, psychological, and social well-being that affects how you think, feel, and act. It helps you handle stress, build relationships, make decisions, and contribute to your community. Good mental health means more than just the absence of illness.

Physical Health

IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Activity/Body

It refers to the condition of your body and how well it functions. It involves maintaining a healthy lifestyle through regular exercise, balanced nutrition, adequate rest, and avoiding harmful habits. Good physical health means having the energy to perform daily activities and reducing the risk of illness or injury.
