Understanding Addiction

Addiction

Addiction is a complex condition characterized by a compulsive engagement in rewarding stimuli despite adverse consequences. It involves biological, psychological, and social factors.

Deeper Knowledge of Different Types of Addiction

1. Substance Use Disorders

  • Alcohol
  • Cannabis
  • Stimulants (Cocaine, Amphetamines)
  • Opioids (Heroin, Painkillers)
  • Hallucinogens
  • Tobacco
  • Inhalants

2. Behavioral Addictions

  • Gambling
  • Internet & Social Media
  • Gaming
  • Shopping
  • Food
  • Exercise
  • Workaholism

How to Deal with Addictions

  • Do things that you enjoy like painting or singing.
  • Use stress relievers (poppits, stress balls) — you can request your Stress Relief Kit here.
  • Set goals to avoid procrastination.
